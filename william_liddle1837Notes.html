When William Liddle was born on 26 February 1837 in Whitefaugh, 
near Carrington, Midlothian, his father, Alexander, 
was 27 and his mother, Janet, was 28. He married Agnes Archibald 
on 19 December 1862 in Lasswade, Midlothian. They had four children 
during their marriage. 
He died at 10 Burghlee Terrace, Loanhead on 13 October 1900 
at the age of 63.
