John Grant was born on 28 September 1858 in Boharm, Banffshire. His father John was 38 and his mother Isabella was 29. He married Mary Ritchie on 27 July 1888. They had six children in 17 years. For many years he was the ghillie and gamekeeper at Delfur Estate on the river Spey until he retired to Mossend, Mulben. He then went to live with his daughter Net and her husband in Shalloch. He died on 23 April 1939 in Banffshire at the age of 80.
