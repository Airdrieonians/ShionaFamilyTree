When Jessie Anne (Nan) Grant was born on 22 December 1899 in Boharm,
Banffshire, her father, John, was 41, and her mother, Mary, was 29. 
She had one daughter Jemima (Mamie) from an unknown father. 
She travelled to Edinburgh to give up her daughter to be fostered at 
a few days old. 
Later she married William David Cruickshank and they had 
two children together. 
She died in 1960 at the age of 61.
