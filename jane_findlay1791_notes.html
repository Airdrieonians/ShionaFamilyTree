When Jane Findlay was born on 5 January 1791 
in Bellie, Moray, her father, Alexander, was 21, 
and her mother, Jannet, was 16. 
She married William Grant on 23 January 1820. 
They had four children during their marriage. 
She died in March 1828 in Moray at the age of 37,
and was buried in Rothes, Moray.
