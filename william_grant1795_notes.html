William Grant was born on 18 April 1795 in Greens, Botriphnie, Banffshire. 
His father Alexander was 30 and his mother Janet was 29. 
He married Jane Findlay and they had four children together. 
He then married Isobel Elder and they had nine children together. 
He died on 24 October 1856 in Boharm, Banffshire, at the age of 61.
